New people, new ages

I've met my "new family", a new unknown horizon.

I've met some of my new college buddies and I think that starting university is going to be a whole new chapter. It's exciting and a bit scary at the same time — new faces, new routines, new challenges. But I'm ready for it.

There's something about meeting people who don't know your past that gives you a chance to reinvent yourself. I want to make the most of this opportunity, to grow, to learn, and to build meaningful connections.

Here's to new beginnings.
