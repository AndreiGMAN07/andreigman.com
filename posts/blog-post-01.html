My first blog entry

A short intro about why I started writing here.

Hello, I am Andrei aka GMAN and I want to tell you why I created this website. Honestly I wanted to have an excuse to stop procrastinating in my free time :). I also want this to mark a new beginning in my life—a new chapter. With this project, I will learn how to maintain a long-term commitment, how to constantly bring something genuine to the table, and how to help both others and myself. It is a bit challenging, but fun. Let's see how this journey goes!.
