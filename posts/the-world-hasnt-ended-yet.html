The World hasn't ended yet

Everything seems to move on into a good direction. I haven't been that happy for a whole year

I am so happy that finally I could feel the holiday as normal people do. I am relaxing, I made some new friends. We created a Discord server and a Minecraft server as well. These days I am relaxing, watching movies, playing video-games, working at my website and I have started to learn for driving school. I am feeling that I could handle all of them. I have not been that happy, like I am right now, since last year. After 6 horror months (December 2025 to July 2026) of burnouts, isolation and depression: so I can feel alive again, the world has not ended yet as Mark Borja from Until Then once said.
